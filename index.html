Welcome to CSBakery

CSBakery is a world renowned maker of cake and pastries. We are the go to store for desserts, birthday cakes, and much more.

Founded in 2020 by high school student Ren Jie Zheng, what was originally a small corner store in Mississauga has grown to become a worldwide chain of bakeries. With over 5000 locations around the globe, CSBakery can be found in almost every major city. If we're not in your area yet, you can bet that we will be soon!
